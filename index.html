Shodh Shikhar

( Igniting Minds, Inspiring Innovation )

In Shodh Shikhar is a flagship annual research compendium and festival organized by the AISECT Group of Universities for the past four years, proudly hosted at Rabindranath Tagore University (RNTU), Bhopal.

Celebrating the spirit of curiosity, innovation, and academic excellence, Shodh Shikhar has emerged as a vibrant platform for young researchers across India to showcase their research contributions and innovations. The event aligns with the national vision of building a developed and self-reliant India, encouraging research that drives socio-economic growth and technological advancement.

Innovation & Commercialization

A key highlight of Shodh Shikhar is its strong emphasis on applied research and real-world impact, providing a unique platform for young innovators and researchers to showcase their projects with commercialization potential.

Each year, researchers from diverse disciplines present innovative prototypes, models, and research-driven solutions that address contemporary challenges in areas such as technology, healthcare, agriculture, education, environmental sustainability, and social entrepreneurship.

Features of Research Project Showcasing at Shodh Shikhar:

  • Dedicated Project Demonstration Zones:
    Spaces for participants to demonstrate their research prototypes, working models, and innovations to a live audience including academic faculty, industry experts, investors, and fellow researchers.
  • Interdisciplinary Project Categories:
    Categories include Engineering & Technology, Agriculture, Medical Innovations, Science Communication, and Industry-Academia Collaboration.
  • Live Feedback from Industry and Academia:
    Constructive feedback and mentorship from industrial partners, investors, AIC-RNTU incubator, and senior academicians to refine ideas for market readiness.

Commercialization and Startup Opportunities

Product/Idea Pitch Sessions:
Selected researchers get the opportunity to pitch their research innovations and business ideas to a panel of investors, startup mentors, and incubation experts, opening doors for funding support and incubation opportunities.

Linkages with AIC-RNTU Incubation Ecosystem:
Through strategic partnerships with entities like the Atal Incubation Centre - RNTU Foundation (AIC-RNTU) and AISECTâ€™s Entrepreneurship Development Cell, researchers are provided with post-event support for prototype development, patent filing, market testing, and scaling.

Recognition and Awards:
Outstanding projects with high commercialization potential are recognized through special awards, including Best Innovation for Social Impact, Best Research Commercialization Idea, and Industry Choice Awards.

Impact So Far:

  • Each year, more than 100 research projects are showcased, spanning social innovation, technology solutions, agricultural advancements, healthcare interventions, and educational tools.
  • Several past participants have converted their research ideas into startups, with ongoing technical mentoring and funding support through AISECTâ€™s innovation network.

Shodh Shikhar continues to serve as a vital platform for connecting research with real-world applications, nurturing a culture where academic inquiry leads to tangible social, economic, and technological outcomes.
