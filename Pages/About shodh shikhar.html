About Us

Shodh Shikhar

( Igniting Minds, Inspiring Innovation )

In Shodh Shikhar is a flagship annual research compendium and festival organized by the AISECT Group of Universities for the past four years, proudly hosted at Rabindranath Tagore University (RNTU), Bhopal.

Celebrating the spirit of curiosity, innovation, and academic excellence, Shodh Shikhar has emerged as a vibrant platform for young researchers across India to showcase their research contributions and innovations. The event aligns with the national vision of building a developed and self-reliant India, encouraging research that drives socio-economic growth and technological advancement.

What Makes Shodh Shikhar Unique?

Shodh Shikhar is not just a research conference — it’s a festival of ideas and collaboration, designed to:

  • Promote a strong research culture within universities
  • Encourage young scholars and innovators to contribute towards national development goals
  • Recognize and reward outstanding research efforts by youth from diverse academic backgrounds
  • Provide a forum to bridge conventional and advanced research domains
  • Commercialization of Research– Industry Academia Collaborations
